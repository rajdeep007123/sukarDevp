About Us

Want the latest fashion in Women, Men and Home from around the World right at your fingertips? Then log onto Sukar.com, the first online private shopping club in the Middle East operating across nine countries, including the GCC, Jordan, Lebanon & Egypt.

In 2012, our leading e-commerce business was acquired by Souq.com and is headquartered in Dubai, UAE with regional offices in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Egypt and Turkey.

Joining is FREE & our 2+ million Members get exclusive access to a large catalog of luxury & lifestyle brands along with our own in-house lines. With our dedicated team of international fashion experts, buyers & customer service agents, we strive to provide an inspiring, convenient & safe online shopping experience.

With ongoing enhancements to our service, we afford our Members the convenience of ordering from their computers, tablets AND smart phones with the option to pay as they want – online or cash on delivery, all with FREE returns.

Everyday we encounter fashion which both inspires & fascinates us. We are passionate about fashion and the individuality of style & expression which we aspire to share through our extensive catalog and daily "Sweet Deals".

Happy Shopping!
